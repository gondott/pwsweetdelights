Meet Our Team

The talented bakers and passionate cookie enthusiasts behind every delicious creation

"Our passion is baking the perfect cookie - one that brings joy, comfort, and a little bit of magic to every bite."

Emma Thompson

Head Baker & Founder

With over 15 years of experience in pastry arts, Emma founded Cookies with a mission to create the perfect balance of chewy and crispy cookies. Her Red Velvet Cookie recipe has won multiple local awards.

Daniel Rodriguez

Pastry Chef

Daniel brings creativity and precision to our kitchen. A graduate from Le Cordon Bleu, he specializes in our brownies and cheesecakes, constantly experimenting with new flavors and techniques to delight our customers.

Sophia Chen

Customer Experience Manager

Sophia ensures that every visit to Cookies is memorable. With her warm personality and attention to detail, she manages our front-of-house operations and specializes in creating custom orders for special occasions.
