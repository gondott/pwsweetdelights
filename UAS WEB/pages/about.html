About Sweet Delights

Our story, our passion, our cookies

Our Story

Sweet Delights Bakery started in 2020 as a small home-based business driven by our founder's passion for baking. What began as weekend cookie sales at the local farmers market quickly grew into a beloved community bakery.

Our journey has been guided by one simple principle: create delicious treats that bring joy to people's lives using only the finest ingredients and time-honored recipes with a modern twist.

Today, we're proud to offer a wide selection of artisanal cookies, brownies, and cheesecakes that continue to delight our customers, while maintaining the same care and attention to detail that went into our very first batch of cookies.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer gluten-free or vegan options?
Yes! We have a growing selection of both gluten-free and vegan cookies. Our Chocolate Chip and Red Velvet cookies are available in gluten-free versions, while our Oatmeal Raisin and Dark Chocolate cookies can be made vegan. Just ask our staff about today's available options.
Can I place custom orders for special events?
Absolutely! We love creating custom cookie arrangements for special occasions. For weddings, corporate events, or any celebration, we can create personalized cookie boxes or platters. Please contact us at least 48 hours in advance for custom orders.
How fresh are your cookies?
All our cookies are baked fresh daily. We never sell cookies that are more than 24 hours old. Any unsold cookies at the end of the day are donated to local food banks and community organizations.
Do you ship your cookies nationwide?
Yes! We offer nationwide shipping with special packaging to ensure your cookies arrive fresh and intact. Standard shipping takes 2-3 business days, and we also offer expedited options. Check our online store for all shipping details.
